What Is Anime?

Anime is a Japanese term for hand-drawn or computer animation. The word is the abbreviated pronunciation of "animation" in Japanese, where this term references all animation. Outside Japan, anime is used to refer specifically to animation from Japan or as a Japanese-disseminated animation style.
Anime consists of an ideal story-telling mechanism, combining graphic art, characterization, cinematography, and other forms of imaginative and individualistic techniques. The production of anime focuses less on the animation of movement and more on the realism of settings as well as the use of camera effects, including panning, zooming, and angle shots. Being hand-drawn, anime is separated from reality by a crucial gap of fiction that provides an ideal path for escapism that audiences can immerse themselves into with relative ease.

Top Recommendations

DEATH NOTE
Light Yagami, a genius high school student discovers a mysterious notebook: the "Death Note", which belonged to the shinigami Ryuk, has the supernatural ability to kill anyone whose name is written in its pages. It involves Light's subsequent attempts to use the Death Note to create a crime-free society, using the alias named "Kira", and the subsequent efforts of an elite Japanese police task force, led by enigmatic detective L, to apprehend him.
DEMON SLAYER
A family is attacked by demons and only two members survive- Tanjiro and his sister Nezuko, who is turning into a demon slowly. Tanjiro sets out to become a demon slayer to avenge his family and cure his sister.
ATTACK ON TITAN
Set in a post-apocalyptic world where the remains of humanity live behind walls protecting them from giant humanoid Titans, Attack on Titan follows protagonist Eren Yeager, along with friends Mikasa Ackerman and Armin Arlert. Eren joins the elite Survey Corps, a group of soldiers who fight against Titans. It chronicles Eren's journey with the Survey Corps as they fight against the Titans while investigating their origin and history.
JUJITSU KAISEN
A boy, Yuji Itadori swallows a cursed talisman- the finger of a demon, Ryomen Sukuna- and becomes cursed himself. He enters a shaman's school to be able to locate the demon's other body parts and thus exorcise himself. Jujutsu Sorcerers are people who control the flow of Cursed Energy in their bodies.
YOUR LIE IN APRIL
Fourteen-year-old piano prodigy Kōsei Arima becomes famous after winning several music competitions. He then loses the ability to perform the piano after his mother's death. The story revolves around his experiences after he meets violinist Kaori Miyazono.
